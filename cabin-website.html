The recently built Japanese inspired 1-bedroom holiday home is located on an acreage in beautiful upper Kaslo approx. 30 minute walk by scenic river trail to downtown. Holiday Home Features include:

  • Combination bath with Japanese washlette.
  • Fully equipped custom cedar kitchen with dish washer and modern appliances.
  • Queen size bed.
  • Front deck with a view of the Purcell Mountains.
